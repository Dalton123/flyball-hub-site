# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

This Cookie Policy explains how Flyball Hub ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at flyballhub.com.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and cannot be disabled.

### Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information such as:

- Pages you visit
- Time spent on each page
- How you arrived at our site
- Your general location (country/city level)

This data helps us improve our website and content. The information is aggregated and anonymous.

### Advertising Cookies

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies to:

- Show ads based on your interests
- Limit the number of times you see an ad
- Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns

Google may use information about your visits to this and other websites to provide relevant advertisements.

## Third-Party Cookies

Our website may contain content from third parties (such as embedded videos or social media widgets) that may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies.

### Google Services

Google AdSense and Google Analytics set cookies on our behalf. For more information about how Google uses data, visit:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google Uses Cookies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies)
- [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

- View what cookies are stored on your device
- Delete individual cookies or all cookies
- Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
- Set preferences for first-party and third-party cookies

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect your experience on our website.

### Opting Out of Personalised Advertising

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting:

- [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
- [Your Online Choices (EU)](https://www.youronlinechoices.eu/)

## Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our site.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
